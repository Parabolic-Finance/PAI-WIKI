Project Overview

Parabolic Agentic Intelligence (PAI) represents a groundbreaking fusion of artificial intelligence and decentralized finance, creating autonomous trading agents that learn, adapt, and execute sophisticated trading strategies without human intervention.

Our platform enables the creation of intelligent trading agents that can analyze market conditions, execute trades, and continuously optimize their performance using advanced machine learning algorithms. Each agent operates independently while contributing to a collective intelligence network.

Agent Copying System

The PAI platform allows users to copy successful agents, democratizing access to sophisticated trading strategies. The copying system enables both novice and experienced traders to benefit from top-performing agents.

How Agent Copying Works:

Users can browse through a marketplace of agents, review their performance metrics, and choose to copy their trading strategies. When you copy an agent:

  • The agent's trades are automatically replicated in your portfolio
  • Position sizes are scaled according to your account balance
  • You maintain full control over your funds
  • You can stop copying at any time

Agent Selection Criteria

When choosing agents to copy, consider:

  • Historical Performance: Returns, consistency, and drawdown periods
  • Risk Level: Volatility and maximum position sizes
  • Strategy Type: Alignment with your investment goals
  • Time Horizon: Short-term vs. long-term strategies
  • Market Focus: Asset classes and geographic markets
